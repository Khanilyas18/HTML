prehistoric era

The bhimbetka rock shelters,a UNESCO World Heritage Site,Showcase evidence of prehistoric human life with painting dating back to around 10,000BCE

Mauryan Empire

Much of present-day Madhya pradesh was part of the mauryan empire,with the empires founder chandragupta maurya conquering northern india around 320 000BCE

Gupta empire

A major portion of central india ,including madhya pradesh was part of the gupta empire from 300 to 550 AD

Harsha reign

During the first half of the 7 th century ,madhya pradesh was under the rule of emperor of harsh reign

Rise of rajput clans

The medeival period saw the the rise of rajput clans like the parmar and chandela with the chandela known for building the khajuraho temples

Medeival period

Muslim rule enterde india ,including central india madhya pradesh,in the early 11 th century ,inluding mahmud ghazni and mohammad ghori who incorporated some area into delhi sultanate

Mughal rule

Almost the entire State came under the mughal empires particularly during the the reign of emperor akbar
